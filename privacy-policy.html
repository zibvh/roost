Privacy Policy

Rooster CBT · com.rooster.cbt · Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Rooster CBT does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

This Privacy Policy explains how Rooster CBT ("the app") handles your information. The app is a JAMB UTME exam practice tool for Android, built for Nigerian secondary school students.

Data We Collect

We collect nothing. Specifically:

  • No account registration or sign-in required
  • No personal information (name, email, phone number) is ever asked for
  • No usage analytics or crash reporting sent to any server
  • No location data collected
  • No device identifiers collected

Local Storage

The app stores your practice history, scores, and settings locally on your device using Android's standard app storage. This data never leaves your device.

Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all locally stored data.

Network Requests

The app makes one type of network request:

  • On launch and every 6 hours, the app checks getrooster.onrender.com for a newer version. No personal data is sent — it is a read-only check.

No other outbound connections are made.

Third Parties

Rooster CBT does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, analytics services, or social media platforms. There are no third parties with access to any data from this app.

Children's Privacy

Rooster CBT is designed for secondary school students, including users under 13. Because the app collects no data whatsoever, it is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future version, the updated policy will be posted at this URL and the "Last updated" date above will reflect the change.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Reach out via the Rooster website.
